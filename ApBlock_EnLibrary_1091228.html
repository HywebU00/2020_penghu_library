About Us

Penghu County Library is a two-storey building, opened on April 23, 2011, with a construction area of 8332.96 square meters, combined with the main building of the Cultural Bureau, performing arts hall, science museum, stadium, Zhong Liyuan and other cultural parishes, to jointly create a cultural park for the county people. The overall design is very modern, lines and colors are simple style, the design above the outer door like a book side by side, also like a CD-ROM side-by-side placement of the shape, conveys the reading from familiar paper gradually into a digital process.

     The first floor of the library is planned for the hall (circulation counter, public art), local literature area, periodical reading area, children's room, office and public facilities area (bathroom, tearoom), etc. On the east side set up a lactation room, with lactation chairs and power supply, etc., for free use by readers. The second floor is a reading (self-study) room, a library area, an audio-visual area, and a public facilities area. The whole building is brightly lit, and when the reader reads indoors, the outdoor greenness is within reach and the environment is very pleasant.

     At present, the whole library have Wi-Fi service, readers as long as carrying 3C electronic supplies, do not have to go through the complicated application process, you can log into the Wi-Fi in any corner of the library, free to travel the internet world! Through this service, the Library hopes to create and enhance the information environment for the people.
